Welcome to PokéInfo

This site is dedicated to inform users about the first genaration Pokémon. Pokémon is a RPG game where you can catch creatures called Pokémon and train them to use them for battle. The first Pokémon game were the Pokémon Red and Pokémon Green version. In the game to win, you must defeat 8 gyms by battling the gym trainers and collecting their badges, after collecting all the 8 badges you must fight the elite four, and only after defeating them you can challange the Pokémon champion whom you must defeat to win the game.
